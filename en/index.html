Welcome

Imagine a land of ice giants, mountains that challenge the sky, and nature so pure it leaves you breathless. That's Patagonia, a territory stretching to the ends of the earth, where every landscape is an adventure.

From the mystical Beagle Channel to the majesty of the Perito Moreno Glacier, through the imposing peaks of El Chaltén, the dreamlike landscapes of Torres del Paine, and the white immensity of Antarctica, every corner holds a story and a memory for a lifetime.

Are you ready to explore the end of the world?

Destinations

Ushuaia, the city at the end of the world

View gallery

The southernmost city on the planet awaits you with unique landscapes where mountains, forests, and the sea come together in an unforgettable setting.

From hikes in Tierra del Fuego National Park, to boat trips along the Beagle Channel, and the famous End of the World Train —every corner has something special for you.

In winter, enjoy skiing at Cerro Castor and the most fun snow activities: sled rides, snowmobiles, and more!
In summer, live adventures among lakes, glaciers, and incredible wildlife.

Our tours are personalized, designed to adapt to the needs and preferences of every traveler. We want you to take home much more than photos: an authentic, comfortable experience full of unforgettable moments.

We take care of every detail so you can simply enjoy. Ready to discover Ushuaia in a unique way?

El Calafate, the land of glaciers

View gallery

Discover the magic of El Calafate with us.

The heart of Patagonia awaits you with breathtaking landscapes, endless skies, and one of the world’s most impressive natural wonders: the Perito Moreno Glacier.

Walk along scenic boardwalks with unique views, feel the thrill of a boat ride in front of the glacier, or dare to step onto the ice for an unforgettable adventure.

Beyond the town, explore Patagonian estancias, turquoise lakes, and quiet mountains that connect you with nature in its purest form.

Our tours are personalized—designed so you can experience El Calafate at your own pace, with comfort and in your language.

You won’t just take incredible photos… you’ll create memories that will stay with you forever!

We take care of everything—you just bring your desire to live an unforgettable adventure.

El Chaltén, the national trekking capital

View gallery

Experience the magic of El Chaltén with us.

The most adventurous corner of Patagonia awaits you with majestic mountains, unforgettable trails, and the imposing Mount Fitz Roy watching over every step.

Hike through movie-like landscapes, surrounded by forests, crystal-clear rivers, and breathtaking natural viewpoints. Feel the true connection with nature in its purest state.

From gentle walks to challenging treks, El Chaltén offers options for every level.

Our tours are personalized: designed so you can explore at your own pace, with local guides, in your language, and with all the comfort you deserve. Every corner is a postcard and every moment, an adventure!

You relax. We'll handle everything.

Torres del Paine, wonder of southern Chile

View gallery

Torres del Paine: the pure essence of Patagonia. Majestic mountains, ancient glaciers and deep-blue lakes shape one of the most breathtaking landscapes on Earth.

Exploring these trails is more than hiking — it’s about connecting with nature, with silence, and with yourself. Every step reveals a new view and a new emotion.

At Patagonia Tours, we design tailor-made experiences so you can explore this destination with comfort, safety and the sense of wonder that only Patagonia can offer.

We believe the best journeys begin with trust and stay in your heart forever.

Antarctica, a magical destination by nature

View gallery

Antarctica: the end of the world… and beyond. White, vast and silent — Antarctica is a place that truly leaves you speechless.

Travelling there is not just visiting a destination — it’s living a deep, transformative, once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Sail among icebergs, watch penguins and whales in their natural habitat, and feel the purity of the frozen continent. These are moments that stay with you forever.

At Patagonia Tours, we take care of every detail so you can explore with confidence, comfort and the calm of being in expert hands.

Because travelling this far isn’t just reaching a place — it’s reconnecting with what truly matters.

About Me

I am Fernando Perea, a Logistics Technician with over 25 years of experience in the world of tourism and transportation. Throughout my career, I have worked for receptive agencies, transport companies, and in logistics for film shoots and corporate events all across Patagonia. From every experience, I learned something valuable: that trips are not just planned; they are felt, lived, and remembered.

That is how Patagonia Tours & Travel was born, an official Travel and Tourism Agency registered with the RNAV (Argentina's National Registry of Travel Agencies). My passion is designing customized proposals so that every traveler can discover the most incredible destinations at the end of the world with confidence, warmth, and safety. We create unique experiences in Ushuaia, El Calafate, El Chaltén, Torres del Paine, and Antarctica, blending nature, adventure, and the human touch that makes all the difference.

If you are thinking of traveling to the deep south, it will be my pleasure to guide you.
Because getting to know Patagonia isn't just a trip... it's an emotion that stays with you forever.
